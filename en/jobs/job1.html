Job description 1

We are looking for an experienced concrete element workers to our clients factories.

Our clients are the largest precast concrete operators in Finland, whose factories are located throughout Finland. We are looking for people with experience in production: wall elements, sandwich panels, floor slabs, etc. Knowledge and ability to read blueprints is preferred.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in concrete panel work
  • Good English skills
  • EU citizenship or work permit in Finland


We offer you:

  • Salary according to TES, based on experience and skills
  • Interesting and diverse tasks and projects
  • High-quality equipment
  • Occupational health care
  • Professional support and advice in employment matters


Fill out the recruitment form and tell us about yourself and your previous experience, and we will contact you!
